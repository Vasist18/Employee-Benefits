General Retirement Plans

  • 401(k) Plan - A tax-advantaged retirement savings plan. More on this
  • Roth IRA - An individual retirement account that allows tax-free growth. More on this
  • Pension Plan - A fixed sum paid regularly to a person after retirement. More on this
  • Simple IRA - A retirement plan that allows employees to contribute through payroll deductions. More on this
  • SEP IRA - A Simplified Employee Pension plan allowing employers to make contributions to their employees' retirement savings. More on this
  • Defined Benefit Plan - A retirement plan where employer provides a specified payment amount in retirement. More on this
  • Target Date Fund - A mutual fund that automatically reallocates assets as the target date approaches. More on this
  • Cash Balance Plan - A type of defined benefit plan that provides a "cash balance" account. More on this
